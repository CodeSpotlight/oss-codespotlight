About the Program

The CodeSpotlight Open Source Internship Program is designed to offer developers an official internship experience focused on collaboration, learning, and building tangible projects in the open-source ecosystem. Contribute to meaningful repositories, enhance your technical and collaboration skills, and receive recognition for your efforts.

Whether you're a frontend enthusiast, backend developer, or documentation expert, this program is tailored to nurture your abilities while working on technologies like Angular, Next.js, and Node.js.

Program Highlights

  • Flexible Duration: Complete the internship in 2 to 6 months, based on your availability. Work at your own pace and balance it with your existing commitments.
  • Open Source Focus: All contributions revolve around open source, fostering real-world experience in version control, collaboration, and modern development workflows.
  • Multiple Tracks: Choose from available tracks like Frontend - Angular, Frontend - Next.js, or Backend - Node.js, with more tracks scheduled for future phases.
  • Certification: Receive an official internship certificate that highlights your contributions, tech stack, and key accomplishments.

Why CodeSpotlight?

CodeSpotlight is unique because it bridges the gap between open-source contributions and formal internships. Open source is typically unpaid, but incredibly valuable for real-world experience. This program formalizes that process, offering structure and credibility while empowering you to collaborate, create, and learn in a community-driven environment.

Contributions of any kind are welcome, even if you’re not officially an intern. At CodeSpotlight, your coding journey matters, whether you fix a bug, contribute documentation, or tackle a key feature as part of the program.
